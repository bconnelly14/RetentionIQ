# RetentionIQ
RetentionIQ is an AI-driven student retention platform designed to help universities proactively identify and support at-risk students before disengagement becomes irreversible.

The platform aggregates data from sources like LMS activity, attendance, and academic performance to generate a dynamic risk score for each student. More importantly, it goes beyond simple flagging—RetentionIQ explains why a student is at risk and provides actionable, advisor-focused recommendations to guide timely intervention.

Built with a focus on usability and impact, the system enables advisors and student success teams to:

Detect early warning signs of disengagement
Understand the key factors driving student risk
Prioritize outreach based on urgency and impact
Track interventions and measure outcomes over time

RetentionIQ shifts student success efforts from reactive to proactive, using data and AI to improve retention, student outcomes, and institutional efficiency.
